## Usage Guidelines

### Hierarchy
- Use consistent heading levels (don't skip H2 to H4)
- Limit to 2-3 type sizes per view for visual clarity
- Use weight and color, not just size, to create hierarchy

### Readability
- Body text: 45-75 characters per line
- Paragraph spacing: 1em minimum
- Sufficient contrast for accessibility (WCAG AA minimum)

### Responsive Considerations
- Scale Display and H1 down on mobile (0.8x multiplier)
- Maintain Body size for readability
- Adjust line heights for smaller screens
